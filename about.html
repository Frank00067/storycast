Our story

We believe in the power of audio

StoryCast started in 2022 as a one-person passion project. Today it's a small collective of writers, sound designers, and editors — united by a love of storytelling.

Why we exist

Our Mission

We exist to give independent audio storytellers a platform with no algorithmic gatekeeping. Every episode is curated by hand. Every story is given the space it needs to breathe. We are listener-supported and will never run ads.
